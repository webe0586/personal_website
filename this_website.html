THIS WEBSITE

In the beginning...

The website began when I was editing my resume and realizied I was doing way to much to ever get it down to one page. There are so many cool projects and experiences I want everyone to know about, so I couldn't decide what to leave off of my resume: then came this idea. I don't know what specifically made me think of it, but suddenly, I had a solution. I could have a short resume and still be able to show off my experiences, and in a format that shows I have experience with web design: a personal website!
